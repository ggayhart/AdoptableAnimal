Chicken Pot Pie

Chicken Pot Pie was brought to the animal shelter as a stray.
I originally came to SVASC as a stray that went unclaimed and am believed to be a 1 year old, Pit Bull / Boxer mix. I'm a super friendly and outgoing pup with lots of awesome Pittie energy. I love going for walks, but do need some work on my leash manners. I'm a very smart guy, though so I think I'll pick up on good leash habits quickly. I would like to find either an active family or a home that has a yard that I can play in. I do get along well with other dogs, but play rough, so please keep that in mind when considering adding me to your family. I am also good with kids, but don't always understand my size, so I may knock someone very young over. I would love to meet all members of the family before going home.
